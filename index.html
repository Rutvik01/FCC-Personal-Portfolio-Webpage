ABOUT


Hi, I’m Tom and I’m the founder and owner of The Happy Pad Company. I’ve always wanted to start a business and The Happy Pad Company is my first venture that I have turned from an idea into a reality. I’m always learning about how to become more productive and how to use technology in a way that makes things run more efficiently. I’m also really good at Microsoft Excel.

The Happy Pad Company is launching in early 2018, and we are a growing business that offers fun stationery and gifts at affordable prices. Serving customers across the United Kingdom, we provide a wide variety of items – from notepads and pencil cases to mugs and water bottles.

We also offer fantastic customer service, ensuring that the process of ordering from us is as simple as possible and making sure that any questions that you have are answered accurately and with integrity.

Our website is happypad.co. Take a look, we’ve got some amazing stuff there!
